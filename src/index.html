No cash, payment is automatic

High income with guaranteed payments
We pay wages to the driver’s personal bank card! You do not need to register an LLC, individual entrepreneur, pay taxes, file a declaration, pay for accounting services and maintain a bank account.
Comfortable, transparent and simple connection
We connect remotely and free of charge, you do not need to come to us! You send us copies of documents, we register you and send you connection information, in 1-2 days you can start working!
24/7 support

Work at a convenient time, no schedules

Always a large number of orders
The application shows where there are currently the most orders and where increased rates apply. You will not have downtime and idle runs. Our passengers are always pleasant and absolutely non-conflict people who are a pleasure to carry.
Without checkers and stickers

CONDITIONS: with your own car:
  • We connect to the UBER system at 10%
  • No fees 0%!
  • We can connect remotely without coming to the office
  • Orders are distributed without the rating of the nearest our drivers
  • Work only by bank transfer
  • We transfer the money to the driver’s card or cash in the office
CONDITIONS: by company cars:
  • We provide a car without collateral
  • No daily rent payments
  • Wednesday Friday and Saturday work for the company and the rest days you work for yourself
REQUIREMENT:
  • Availability of a car without advertising
  • The car should not be yellow and not with yellow numbers
  • The car is not older than 3 years
  • Driving experience of at least 3 years
  • Device running Android 4.0 and higher (Samsung)
DOCUMENTATION:
  • Driver license
  • Passport front side
  • Vehicle certificate
  • License (if any)
  • Phone number
  • Card number for transferring money (indicate in the letter)!
